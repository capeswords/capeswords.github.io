Adult Sports Fencing

The interplay of lightning-quick movements and fast-changing tactics where the fastest and smartest sword wins in a flash of a blade. Fencing is a formal modern sport dating back to the 17th century and has been in every modern Olymic Games since 1896. Perfect to sharpen and improve ones mind and body no matter your age. In our club we train in the foil, sabre and épée

HEMA

Historical European Martial Arts is the modern revival of Europe's lost martial history and traditions. Here we learn to fight with medieval, renaissance and victorian weapons. From longswords, daggers, military sabre's to quarterstaff's and halberds. Just as lightning fast as sports fencing demanding more control and combined with weapons that fight and feel closer to actual weapons. A perfect sport for all ages
